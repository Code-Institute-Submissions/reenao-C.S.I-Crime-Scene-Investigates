Facts

C.S.I is not shot in Las Vegas

C.S.I is shot using skylines and sets in Santa Clarita, Calfornia and surrounding area. Occasionally filming in Las Vegas.

The C.S.I's are scientists not members of Police Department.

To Introduce C.S.I Miami a cross-jurisation episode was used.

A case starting in Las Vegas led them to Miami. As Miami team introduced themselves to Las Vegas team the audience was getting to know them.

Theme Tune is "Who are you?

C.S.I Effects--

Is when they show the camera going down into a wound track or deeper into something like going into aeroplane workings. They have also shown what a ball hitting the heart will do. the csi effect is how csi is different and standout from other murder shows.

William Peterson as Gil Grissom:

Is a forensic entomologist and has a doctoral degree in biology from University Of California. Gil was the shift supervisor on graveyard. He became a C.S.I in 1985 and left Las Vegas Crime lab in 2009. He was a researcher for a short time. Gil became a sea advocate and rejoined ex wife Sara. They sail off together to the Port of San Diego.

Marg Helgenberger as Catherine Willows

Catherine is a blood splatter analyst. She is assistant supervisor on graveyard shift. She worked for a while as graveyard supervisor. Catherine was demoted after a departmental scandel and join F.B.I as a special agent.In the series finale Catherine returns and gets directorship of the crime lab.
